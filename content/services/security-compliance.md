## What We Deliver

- **Security audits** of infrastructure, code, and CI/CD pipelines
- **Compliance frameworks** (SOC 2 Type II, HIPAA, PCI-DSS, ISO 27001)
- **IAM & secrets management** (AWS IAM, Vault, SOPS)
- **Encryption at rest & in transit** (KMS, TLS, certificate management)
- **Incident response planning** and post-mortem processes

## Why It Matters

Security isn't optional — especially if you handle customer data or need investor diligence. We help you pass audits, win enterprise deals, and sleep better at night.

## Typical Engagement

- **Week 1:** Security audit and gap analysis
- **Week 2-4:** Implement controls, harden infrastructure
- **Week 5-6:** Document policies, train team
- **Week 7+:** Continuous monitoring and compliance maintenance
