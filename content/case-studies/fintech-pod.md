**Challenge**
Understaffed engineering team needed new features, performance gains, and reliability.

**What We Did**
- Cross-functional pod under TrustD coordination
- BE APIs (Node.js) + legacy refactors
- FE flows (React) + testing
- QA automation (Playwright) integrated in CI
- Data pipelines feeding analytics dashboards

**Outcome**
- 2× release cadence within two sprints
- QA coverage from 25% → 80% critical paths
- 60% fewer post-release incidents
